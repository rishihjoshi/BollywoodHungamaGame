How to Play

The Setup

Enter 3–12 player names. The app secretly assigns everyone a Sholay character. 1–3 players are Daku (villains), the rest are Gaon (heroes). Nobody knows who else is Daku!

Role Reveals

Pass the phone around. Each player taps to reveal their secret character — then memorises it and passes the phone. Don't show your screen to anyone.

Word Reveals

Pass around again. Gaon players see the full secret Bollywood word. Daku players only see the category — they must blend in and fake it!

The Game (No App Needed)

  • Each player says one word as a clue.
  • Daku tries to fake a plausible clue without knowing the word.
  • Discuss as a group, then vote on who the Daku is.

Scoring

  • Gaon wins if all Dakus are correctly identified.
  • Daku wins if even one Daku survives the vote.

Daku Count

  • 3–5 players → 1 Daku (Gabbar)
  • 6–8 players → 2 Dakus (Gabbar + Sambha)
  • 9–12 players → 3 Dakus (Gabbar + Sambha + Kaalia)
