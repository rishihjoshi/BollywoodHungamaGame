How to Play

🎬 The Setup

Enter 3–12 player names. The app secretly assigns everyone a Sholay character. 1–3 players become Daku (villains like Gabbar), the rest are Gaon (heroes). Nobody knows who else is what!

📱 One Pass — Role + Word Together

Pass the phone to each player one at a time. Each player taps "Reveal Role & Word" to see both their character and the secret word on the same screen.

  • Gaon players see their Sholay character and the full secret Bollywood word.
  • Daku players see their character and only the word's category — they must bluff!

Memorise it, tap "Done — Pass Phone", and hand it to the next person. Don't show your screen to anyone.

🗣️ The Game (No App Needed)

  • Each player says one word as a clue related to the secret word.
  • Daku must give a convincing clue without knowing the actual word.
  • Discuss as a group, then vote on who the Daku is.

🏆 Scoring

  • Gaon wins if all Dakus are correctly identified.
  • Daku wins if even one Daku escapes the vote.

💀 Daku Count

  • 3–5 players → 1 Daku (Gabbar)
  • 6–8 players → 2 Dakus (Gabbar + Sambha)
  • 9–12 players → 3 Dakus (Gabbar + Sambha + Kaalia)

How to Play

🎬 What is Picture Abhi Baaki Hai?

One player is the Pitcher. They get a random combo of 4 cards and must improvise a Bollywood movie pitch using all four elements. Everyone else votes for the best pitch. The most-voted pitcher scores points. Most points after all rounds wins.

🃏 The Combo

  • 🎬 Actor — A Bollywood actor to feature in your film.
  • 📍 Location — Where your story is set.
  • 🎭 Genre — The mood and style of the film.
  • ⚡ Wildcard — A twist, dialogue, decade, or co-star constraint that must appear in your pitch.

🎬 How a Round Works

  1. The Pitcher studies their 4-card combo.
  2. Hit Start Pitching — the timer begins. Pitch your Bollywood blockbuster using all 4 elements before time runs out.
  3. When done (or time's up), everyone else votes for the pitch they liked best. Each non-pitcher votes once.
  4. When all votes are in, the host taps Lock In Votes to reveal the winner.

🏆 Scoring

  • The pitcher with the most votes scores points equal to their vote count.
  • On a tie, each tied pitcher gets 1 point.
  • The player with the most points after all rounds is the Bollywood Champion.

⚙️ Setup Options

  • Players: 3–12
  • Rounds: 3, 5 (default), or 7
  • Pitch Timer: 60s (Fast), 90s (Recommended), 120s (Full drama)

💡 Tips

  • You don't have to make it good — make it entertaining.
  • Use the Wildcard early so you don't forget it.
  • Dramatic pauses win votes.
