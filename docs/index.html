How to Play

The Goal

  • The Guesser tries to figure out the secret Bollywood word from clues.
  • Some players (Nice) want the Guesser to succeed. One player (Naughty) wants to confuse them!

Each Round

  1. One player is randomly picked as the Guesser.
  2. Everyone else secretly sees their role — Nice or Naughty.
  3. A secret Bollywood word is revealed to all non-Guessers (Guesser looks away).
  4. Each non-Guesser enters one word as a clue — privately, one at a time.
  5. Duplicate clues are automatically removed.
  6. The Guesser sees the surviving clues and picks from 6 options.
  7. If correct, the Guesser names who they think was Naughty.

Scoring

EventPoints
Guesser guesses correctly+2
Guesser catches the Naughty player+1 bonus
Nice player's clue survives (not cancelled)+1
Naughty player is NOT caught+2

Clue Rules

  • One word only — no spaces allowed.
  • You cannot say the secret word itself.
  • If two players give the same clue, both are cancelled!

End of Game

  • Everyone is Guesser exactly twice. Most points wins!
